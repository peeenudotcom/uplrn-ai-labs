Help me write a professional email. To: [recipient and their relationship to me] Purpose: [e.g., requesting a meeting / following up / sending a report / apologizing for a delay / requesting a recommendation] Key points to include: [list 3–5] Tone needed: [formal / semi-formal / warm / assertive] Length: [short under 100 words / medium / detailed] Give me 2 versions: one direct and one more diplomatic. Also suggest 3 subject line options.

Pro Tip: For important emails, ask "What could go wrong with this email? What might the reader misunderstand?" — fixing issues before sending prevents miscommunication.

Prompt 50
Learning Roadmap Creator
Create a personal learning roadmap for me to develop skills in: Skill/field: [e.g., AI tools / digital marketing / data analysis / public speaking / financial planning / Python programming] Current level: [complete beginner / some basics / intermediate] Time available: [30 min / 1 hour / 2 hours per day] Goal: [get a job / freelance / personal growth / start a business] Timeline: [3 months / 6 months / 1 year] Include: weekly milestones, free + paid resources (Indian context), projects to build, and how to measure progress. Be realistic, not motivational-poster vague.

Pro Tip: Ask to include "one Indian community or forum to join" per skill — learning with peers (WhatsApp groups, Discord, LinkedIn communities) accelerates progress 5x.
